Rei kawakubo
川久保玲

Kawakubo Rei majored in the Philosophy Department and Aesthetic Art History of
the Literature Faculty at Keio University in Japan.
After she graduate, she joined the Japanese textile company for marketing, but
retired after three years and became a independent freelance stylist.
She couldn't find clothes that fit the image she wanted while she was working as a
photographer. That lead her to make clothes on her own.
She founded Comme des Garçons(1973), a luxury women's ready-to-wear brand and
held it's first show in Tokyo(1975).
