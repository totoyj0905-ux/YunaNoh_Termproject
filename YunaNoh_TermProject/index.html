Welcome.

Comme des Garçons (French for 'Like Boys', also CDG for short). A Japanese fashion label based in Paris, founded by Rei Kawajubo. Its French flagship store is located in Paris, while its other physical retail stores are in London, Berlin, Melbourne, Seoul, Hong Kong, New York City and in the Aoyama district of Tokyo. It is now trying to project an intense image on their output by getting out of traditional and folkloric sentiment and getting to know what have been so far to perceive the representative fashion trend for the last few years.
